## Canu Vanu

1. The boss will mark 2 players
2. Totems will spawn on marked players
3. A large purple circle will spawn
4. Pick up the totems and move them out of the circle
5. Repeat until the boss is dead

<OnlyHealer>
    <Trigger color="orange" type="on cast" trigger="Morrowcloud ">
        **Healer**: Cleanse the **Bleed** debuff
    </Trigger>
</OnlyHealer>
