## Tural Vidraal: Valigarmanda

<Alert icon="warning">
    These attacks can happen at any point during the encounter, and **do not have a cast bar**, so you need to be aware of the boss's movements at all times.

    <Trigger type="if" trigger="Boss circles platform" color="red">
        Move to the center of the platform
    </Trigger>

    <Trigger type="if" trigger="Boss hovers above platform" color="red">
        Move to the back of the platform
    </Trigger>

    <Trigger type="if" trigger="Boss opens mouth" color="red">
        Move to the front sides of the platform to avoid cone
    </Trigger>
</Alert>

## Thunder Phase
The arena will become a grid of tiles; glowing tiles will cause the player to **levitate**.

<Trigger type="on cast" trigger="Thunderous Breath" color="orange">
    Move onto a glowing tile
</Trigger>

<Trigger type="when" trigger="Feathers land on platform" color="orange">
    Move off the glowing tiles **but** not near a feather<Consequence> because they will explode in a small AoE</Consequence>
</Trigger>

<Trigger type="when" trigger="Arcane Spheres spawn" color="orange">
    Move to row where there is no sphere
</Trigger>

## Ice Phase
<Trigger type="when" trigger="Avalanche text shows" color="blue">
    Move out of the way of indicated avalanche (look behind you!)
</Trigger>

<Trigger type="on cast" trigger="Freezing Dust" color="blue">
    Make sure you are moving when the timer runs out<Consequence> otherwise you will be frozen in place</Consequence>
</Trigger>

<Trigger type="on" trigger="Circles spawn" color="blue">
    Get in position to dodge cardinal + intercardinal line AOEs
</Trigger>

## Neutral Phase
<Trigger type="on cast" trigger="Ruinfall" color="red">
    <OnlyTank>
        **Tank**: Soak tankbuster tower with other tank
    </OnlyTank>

    <AllExceptTank>
        **DPS & Healers**: Telegraphed knockback; position yourself to prevent getting knocked into AOEs at the back of the platform
    </AllExceptTank>
</Trigger>

<Trigger type="when" trigger="Beacons spawn" color="red">
    Kill beacons quickly<Consequence> to prevent filling the boss's <Red>**Ruinous Power**</Red> gauge, which will cause a **wipe** if filled</Consequence>

    Dodge cone AOEs and **stack** on constant stack line indicators
</Trigger>
